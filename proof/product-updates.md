## Product Voice Notes

When writing about new features, always:
- Lead with **what the customer can now do**, not what we built
- Use **before/after framing** where possible ("you used to have to X, now you can Y")
- Avoid: "We're excited to announce...", technical implementation details, roadmap speculation
