# Marketing Strategy

This file captures your actual marketing approach so the AI can plan content that fits how you operate — not some hypothetical ideal. Be honest about what you actually do, not what you aspire to do. A realistic strategy file produces better plans than an aspirational one.

The AI uses this when you ask it to plan a campaign, suggest a content calendar, prioritize channels, or think about sequencing.
