## Writing Style Rules

- **Sentence length:** Prefer short. Mix in longer ones for rhythm. Max 25 words for key points.
- **Paragraphs:** 2–3 sentences max in digital content.
- **Active vs. passive:** Always active. "We built X" not "X was built."
- **Numbers:** Use digits (7, not seven) for anything 10 and above.
