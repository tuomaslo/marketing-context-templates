# Brand Core
**Bucket:** Identity — included in every AI prompt automatically

---

## Mission
What we do and why it matters:

> [YOUR BRAND] helps [TARGET AUDIENCE] [achieve specific outcome] so they can [deeper benefit].

**Example:** "Acme helps B2B marketing teams produce on-brand content 10x faster so they can focus on strategy, not copy."

---

## Vision
Where we're going in 3–5 years:

> [What the world looks like when your company succeeds at scale]

**Example:** "A world where every marketing team has an AI that truly understands their brand — and never makes them cringe."

---

## What We Do
Core offering in 2–3 sentences. Avoid jargon. Write this as if explaining to a smart friend outside your industry.

[YOUR BRAND] is a [category — e.g., "marketing AI platform / GTM analytics tool / content automation system"].

We [primary action — e.g., "generate on-brand social posts, emails, and ad copy"] by [mechanism — e.g., "connecting to your brand context, past content, and live data sources"].

Our customers use us to [primary use case — e.g., "ship 3x more content without hiring more people"].

---

## Who We Serve
Primary audience in one sentence:

> [Job title(s)] at [company type/size] who [defining characteristic or struggle].

**Example:** "Head of Marketing and Content Leads at B2B SaaS companies (50–500 employees) who are small teams expected to punch above their weight."

---

## Core Values
3–5 values that genuinely guide decisions (not aspirational fluff):

| Value | What it means in practice |
|-------|--------------------------|
| [Value 1, e.g., Directness] | [e.g., We say what we mean. No corporate speak in our products, our content, or our team.] |
| [Value 2, e.g., Customer obsession] | [e.g., We build what customers ask for. We kill features nobody uses.] |
| [Value 3, e.g., Quality over quantity] | [e.g., We'd rather ship one great thing than three mediocre ones.] |

---

## Brand Promise
The single sentence a customer should remember after interacting with you:

> "[YOUR BRAND] means [concrete, specific promise — not a vague feeling]."

**Example:** "Acme means your pitch always lands — because we only send you leads worth your time."

---

## One-Line Elevator Pitch
The version you'd say at a conference in 10 seconds:

> "[YOUR BRAND] is [category] for [audience] that [does X] — [differentiator]."
