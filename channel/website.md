## Blog Post Structure

### SEO/Informational post
```
H1: [Keyword-rich, specific — not clever, clear]

[Intro: 2–3 paragraphs]
  - Hook: state the problem or question readers came for
  - Promise: what they'll know or be able to do after reading
  - Brief credibility: why we're qualified to answer this

H2: [First major section]
  [Content — 3–6 paragraphs or a structured list]

H2: [Second major section]
  [Content]

H2: [Third+ sections as needed]

H2: [Conclusion]
  - Key takeaways (bulleted)
  - CTA — specific next step (not just "contact us")
```

### Thought leadership post
```
H1: [Opinionated title — take a clear position]

[Hook: the specific claim or observation this piece is built on]

[Setup: why this matters now / what's changing]

[Body: your argument — ideally 3–5 supporting points with evidence or examples]

[Counterargument: acknowledge what you're leaving out or where you could be wrong]

[Conclusion: restate your position, add a forward-looking point]

[CTA: invite discussion, subscribe, or try the product]
```

## Landing Page Structure

### Product/feature landing page:
```
ABOVE THE FOLD:
  H1: [Benefit-focused headline — what they get, not what we do]
  Subhead: [Clarify or expand the headline — who it's for + how]
  CTA button: [Action word] → [Outcome] (e.g., "Start free" / "See it in action")

  [Make them feel understood before pitching]

  [Scannable list — benefit first, then feature]

  [Repeat main CTA — different framing]
```

## Writing Style Notes for Web

- **Headlines:** Clear beats clever. "How to Cut Content Production Time by 60%" beats "Work Smarter, Not Harder"
- **Subheads:** Should tell the story on their own — skimmers read only H2s
- **Intro paragraph:** State the problem in the reader's language within the first 3 sentences
- **CTA copy:** Action + outcome ("Start writing on-brand content in 20 minutes") beats generic ("Get started")
- **Internal links:** Every post should link to 2–3 related pages and one conversion-oriented page

---

## Things to Avoid on Website

- **Homepage that talks about us before talking about the reader's problem** — flip it
- **"Learn more" as CTA copy** — say what they'll get when they click
- **Blog posts that could have been 500 words but are padded to 2,000** — quality over length
- **[Your specific issue 1]** — [Why]
- **[Your specific issue 2]** — [Why]
